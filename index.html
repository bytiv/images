SVG files and images with titles

How to Add Your Images:

1. Place your image files (SVG, PNG, JPG) in the same folder as this HTML file

2. Copy one of the gallery items below and paste it inside the <div class="gallery">

3. Replace "image.svg" with your image filename

4. Replace "Image Title" with your desired title
